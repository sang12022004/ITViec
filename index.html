Job description

  • Provide functional support for Oracle HR and Finance modules, including troubleshooting and resolving issues to ensure smooth operations.
  • Assist in the configuration and maintenance of Oracle applications to align with business requirements.
  • Collaborate with end-users to understand their needs and provide appropriate solutions or enhancements.
  • Conduct regular system audits to ensure data integrity and compliance with company policies.
  • Develop and maintain documentation related to system configurations, processes, and user guides.
  • Participate in testing and implementation of system updates, patches, and new releases.
  • Provide training and support to end-users to enhance their proficiency with Oracle applications.
  • Participate in cross-functional projects involving Oracle applications, ensuring HR and Finance module alignment with business goals.

Your skills and experience

  • Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, Finance, Human Resources, or a related field.
  • Strong understanding of Oracle HR and Finance modules, with hands-on experience in configuration and support. In particular:
    • Core HR: access rights (user roles and area of responsibilities), approval workflow configuration, legal entity/job/location/department objects management.
    • Absence: absence plans and types configuration, including fast formulas.
    • Recruitment: job requisitions, automatic notification email, contract generation and career site management.
    • Learn: overall module configuration
    • Compensation: compensation review cycle and configuration (models, worksheet configuration, external data)
  • Proficiency in SQL and experience with Oracle reporting tools.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Strong English communication and interpersonal skills to effectively interact with end-users and team members.
